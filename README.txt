The following files are required:

VB version 6.0

MSFLXGRD.OCX

These files are primarily required for the "Load tables" forms.

The client required a system written in Microsoft Access, that would track the movement of diamonds from a central dispatch point to individual offices and back. The clients are allocated offices and as the parcels are distributed out and returned the Allocation form keeps track of their journey and thereby the names of the clients who last viewed them and in which office.
